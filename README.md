# 🚀 SCIP – Smart Community Intelligence Platform

> **AI-Powered Decision Intelligence Platform for Smarter Communities**

SCIP (Smart Community Intelligence Platform) is an AI-driven platform that enables citizens to report community issues while helping local authorities prioritize, analyze, and respond to them using data-driven insights and Generative AI.

Developed for the **Gen AI Academy APAC Edition** by **Google Cloud × Hack2Skill** under the theme **AI for Better Living and Smarter Communities**.

---

# 🌍 Problem Statement

Communities face challenges such as:

* Water supply issues
* Road damage
* Waste management
* Electricity outages
* Public health concerns

Traditional complaint systems often suffer from:

* Slow response times
* Poor prioritization
* Fragmented data
* Limited analytics
* Lack of AI-assisted decision making

---

# 💡 Solution

SCIP transforms community complaints into actionable intelligence using AI.

The platform enables:

* Digital complaint registration
* Complaint lifecycle management
* Community risk assessment
* AI-powered recommendations
* Analytics dashboards
* Executive reporting
* Decision support for authorities
